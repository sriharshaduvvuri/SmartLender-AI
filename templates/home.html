Predictive Algorithms

XGBoost Classifier

Extreme Gradient Boosting decision trees. Fits ensemble models iteratively to correct weak prediction residues. Highly robust against tabular overfitting.

Random Forest

A bootstrap ensemble bagging classifier. Fits many parallel deep decision trees and votes on output classes to minimize prediction variance.

Decision Tree Classifier

A clear, tree-like flowchart logic splits nodes on information gain index values (Gini/Entropy). Highly interpretable structure.

K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)

Instance-based classification that groups application profiles based on geometric distance clusters (Euclidean metric) of closest neighbors.
